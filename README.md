# StayGreenPolymers
Data Visuals Of Google map with Locations of the Customers.
There network is shown in the map with the help of Stream-lit.
What is the use? 
This business is about collecting waste and scarps. 
So, there are lot of shops who sell these scraps, we buy those scraps and do business in bulk orders or Recycle them.
So, it will be hard for the Business owner to remember every shops.
This model is used in order to make that easy and simple to remember also with thier locations of the seller/customers.
